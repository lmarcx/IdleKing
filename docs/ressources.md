### System 
Ressources can be collected through the Adunbance Horn with incremental click. 
Or in specific Building with automatic incremental :
- Farm and Mine only.
Ressources can be transformed through specifi Buildings :
- Kitchen : Transforms Farm ressources into Plates
- Forge : Transforms Mine ressources into Equipments

### Basic Ressources (All from Age I) :
- Stone : Farm
- Wood : Farm
- Water : Farm
- Meat : Farm
- Copper : Mine
- Silver : Mine
- Gold : Mine

### Enhanced Ressources by Buildings by Ages :
