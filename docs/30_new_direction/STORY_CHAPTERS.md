# STORY CHAPTERS

## Narrative Premise

The player embodies a mysterious old king wandering through a shattered dream world.

Without clear memories of his past, he traverses ruined kingdoms, frozen civilizations, drowned lands and broken eras shaped by catastrophic events that ended entire ages of existence.

Accompanied by allies encountered during his journey, he seeks a way to restore balance to the River of Life and save the trapped souls scattered across the dream.

Behind this journey lies a deeper truth: an ancient cosmic conflict, a fractured consciousness falling endlessly into the abyss, and an entity attempting to freeze existence itself to ensure its own survival.

Throughout the story, fragmented visions and subtle memories emerge through the old king’s mind, hinting at a forgotten identity.

---

# Hidden Cosmology

## The Primordial Beginning

At the origin of existence stood two primordial entities:

- **Créa** — embodiment of creation
- **Naan** — embodiment of destruction

They are not gods in the religious sense, but foundational cosmic forces.

Creation and destruction existed in equilibrium.

---

## Amal

Amal was born accidentally from the interaction between:

- Naan’s destructive power
- Créa’s creations

Amal became an anomalous being capable of both creation and destruction.

However:

- Amal cannot directly destroy Créa or Naan
- Amal has limitations against original creations

---

## Chaos

Chaos is Amal’s mirrored counterpart.

Where Amal emerged from:

> Naan’s power within Créa’s creations

Chaos emerged from:

> Créa’s power within Naan’s destruction

Chaos and Amal are conceptual opposites.

---

## The Abyssal Well

Naan eventually used his own destructive power on himself.

This created:

**The Abyssal Well**

A metaphysical infinite abyss formed from:

- Naan’s fractured consciousness
- endless descent
- collapsing timelines
- unstable dream states

Naan continues falling endlessly within this abyss.

---

## The River of Life

The River of Life is the manifestation of Créa’s creative flow entering the Abyss.

It carries:

- souls
- life essence
- fragments of creation

Normal cycle:

Creation → Life → Death → Return → Reassembly → Creation

Current state:

Broken, fragmented, corrupted.

---

## Rêves

Rêves was born from the mixed tears of:

- Nostal
- Naan

Rêves is an oniric metaphysical entity.

Its role is protective.

The dream world exists through Rêves.

---

## The Dream World

The game takes place inside:

**One single dream world**

This world contains multiple eras representing fractured temporal layers.

Canonical chronological order:

1. Affection
2. Affliction
3. Corruption
4. Deluge
5. Glaciation
6. Calcination
7. Funereal
8. Isolation

The player experiences a different progression order.

---

# Waqt

Waqt is a powerful metaphysical antagonist operating inside the Abyss.

Motivations:

- survival
- domination
- fear of extinction
- preventing Naan’s return
- preventing Créa’s intervention

Waqt strengthens his influence through:

- darkness
- stagnation
- corrupted time
- shadow corruption

Inside the dream world, Waqt’s power is partially constrained by Rêves.

---

# The Shadows

Shadows are manifestations of corruption.

They are used by Waqt to:

- corrupt eras
- invade civilizations
- spread darkness
- weaken resistance

The old king can resist shadow corruption because shadows are formed from Naan’s own essence.

---

# The Commanders

Commanders are dream-world beings corrupted by Waqt.

Waqt uses them to destroy eras and extend his control.

Once corrupted, they may be summoned into:

**The Isolation**

There they gather in a hidden Commanders Council.

Purpose:

- receive directives
- discuss strategy
- coordinate corruption efforts
- prepare plans involving the old king

Known commanders:

- Dragon
- Allaeva
- Noah
- Val
- Luvia
- Guerre
- AEN (placeholder)

---

# The Tourments

Created by Amal:

- Cachemir (Nightmare)
- Nostal (Nostalgia)
- Dubi (Doubt)
- Ango (Anxiety)

These are metaphysical incarnations of psychological concepts.

---

# The Player

The player is not directly Naan.

The player is:

**A dream-incarnated projection of Naan**

The old king suffers near-total amnesia.

He occasionally experiences:

- fragmented memories
- visions
- déjà-vu
- subconscious emotional echoes

---

# Tobo

Tobo is a creation of Créa.

Créa placed a fragment of her soul inside Tobo.

Tobo acts as:

- protector
- guide
- Kingdom NPC
- major narrative ally

---

# Companion Direction

Narrative direction:

Hybrid companion system.

Companions may provide:

- narrative support
- assist skills
- passive bonuses
- summons
- support actions

Billy is the first permanent companion.

Additional companions are unlocked through:

- Kingdom reconstruction
- story progression
- encounters across eras
