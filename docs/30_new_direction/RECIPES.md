## Forge Phase 8A

Current Forge recipes and costs are PLACEHOLDER values used to keep the MVP playable while the full recipe tables are designed.

Phase 8A rules:

```txt
Craft spends recipe resources directly.
Upgrade spends placeholder resources / currency directly.
Recycle does not refund recipe resources.
Recycle grants 50% of placeholder item value as ECU.
Recycle has a PLACEHOLDER 20% chance to grant a matching-rarity Precious Stone item.
```
